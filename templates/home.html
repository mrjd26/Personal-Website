About

Mike Jarvis has a passion and interest in science, technology, and engineering. After graduating from the University of Missouri - Kansas City in 2009 with a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Science, Mike joined Harcros Chemicals as a chemical reactor operator, putting the lessons of chemistry to use, applying physical science to the economy of industrial chemical manufacture.

In 2012, Mike sought the technologically-inspired culture of Silicon Valley and joined Tesla Motors battery safety group in Palo Alto, California. His Research and Development efforts supported the safety of the Model S. In 2013, Mike worked on assignment at Google, in a product development role at Google X, a'secret lab' devoted to moonshot projects.

During this time, Mike became a self taught programmer, and applied his learning to enhance collection and analysis of hardware data. His role morphed into a purely software development role on assigment at Google in 2014, providing visibility into Google's worldwide network infrastructure at its Mountain View, California campus headquarters.

Mike along with his wife, Beth and newborn daughter, Claire started 2015 in a new city, Austin, Texas where he continues to grow and learn on his technological journey.
